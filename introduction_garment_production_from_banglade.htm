We would like to introduce ourselves as one of the largest manufacturing group of readymade garments in Bangladesh.

We own two factories viz.,

1) Shanta Garments Limited - 300 Machines - ISO 9002 & C-TPAT Certified

2) Tropica Garments Limited - 300 Machines - WRAP & C-TPAT Certified

Our current annual production capacity is 3 Million pieces for both Bottoms & Tops * for Men’s, Women’s & Children's Wear. Our Product range consists of Pants, Shorts, Shirts, Blouses & Overalls - all woven.

All our 2 manufacturing units have been approved & evaluated by above named buyers to meet their production, quality and safety requirements.

We are currently doing approx. 80% for USA and 20% for Europe.

Shanta Garments Ltd. is qualified for ISO 9001:2000 & C-TPAT certificates are enclosed with the factory profile.

Tropica Garments Ltd. is approved by WRAP & C-TPAT certificates are enclosed with the factory profile.

All of our Factories are able to achieve an A.Q.L. of 2.5 for entire production.

In addition to the above, we have our own Hong Kong Office - Shing On International Limited/Trump Crown (HK) Ltd. which is the Merchandising, Sourcing, Marketing and Designing division of the group. This office places orders for fabrics & trims and follows-up on all orders up to the point of shipment. The group has a Liaison office in Bangladesh, which follows up on Quality Control, Paper pattern, yields and Garments Production.

Brief about our Laundry project:
We have our own In-house Washing Facilities with Waste Water Effluent Treatment Plant and we can handle any type of wash.

Washing Plant which is a union of Management skills and Technical know-how in the specialized area of Garment Washing and Special Finishing. It consists of a team of highly qualified and experienced people of international repute from Philippine and Sri-Lanka.

Washing plant has been set-up in Teajgon Industrial Area, which has all hi-technology equipments to do all kinds of fancy/trendy washes especially in Denims and garment dyeing. List of additional machinery is included in profile of Shanta Washing Plant Ltd.
