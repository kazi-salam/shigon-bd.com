Introduction:
Shanta Washworks Ltd. is a sister concern of Shanta Group, based in Bangladesh with sustained goodwill and expertise in exports oriented ready-made garment manufacturing.

Product:
All types of Woven Tops and Bottom.

Key Specialty:
Denim Apparels with all types of Special Washes.

Daily Capacity:
Shanta’s daily production capacity is approximately 15,000 - 18,000 units, depending on the style.
